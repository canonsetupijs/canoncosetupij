How can I make my offline printer show as online again?


Sometimes the canon printer goes offline when you don't use it more. So to keep the printer always ready for use, keep the printer in use. If by chance the printer is offline, then follow the steps below to get the canon printer online or ready to use again;

  • Firstly on the desktop, click on the Start option of the device.
  • Then in the start option to the right side, you will see the Devices and Printers option.
  • Click on the Devices and Printers option.
  • Then the next page will open.
  • There you will see the printer connected.
  • When you take the curser on the printer, it will show Offline mode.
  • Then Right-click on the printer folder.
  • Then click on See what's printing option from the list of options.
  • After that, the printer status tab will open.
  • There in the upper left corner, click on the Printer option.
  • Then from the list, click on the check the tick on "Use printer offline" option.
  • And also, check the tick on the "Pause printing" option.
  • Then close that tab and Refresh the whole by right-clicking.
  • Again refresh the window.
  • Then again, take the cursor of the mouse over the printer.
  • Then you will see the Ready option.
  • It means the printer is online or ready to use.
  • In this way, the process canon printer online from offline is all set.

  • In the start option, search for Printers and Scanners.
  • Then the option will appear; click on it.
  • Further click on Add devices option.
  • Then the Canon printer will be seen in the devices.
  • Click on the device to add it.
  • That's it, and the cannon.com IJ setup printer is connected wirelessly to a Windows device.

What are the steps to connect cannon.com ijsetup printer Manually to Windows?

Follow the steps below to connect the canon printer manually;


  • Take a USB cable and connect one end of it to the printer.
  • Take the other end of the USB cable and connect it to the windows device.
  • Then turn on the printer and windows device.
  • After that, on the desktop of the windows device, click on the Start option.
  • Further, go for Printer and Scanners search.
  • Then in the printer and scanners, go for Add device option.
  • Then the connected canon printer will appear.
  • Click on Add option to add the Canon printer online.
  • Then the device's manual addition is successful.
